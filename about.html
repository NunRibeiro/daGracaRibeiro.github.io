Nuno da Graça Ribeiro

Nuno da Graça Ribeiro is a recently graduated architect from School of Porto. Since a tender age, Graça Ribeiro loved to play with Legos, subconsciously trying to shape the world he lived in. In the panorama of city and life simulating games, he spent most of the time in the act of building instead of actually simulating a life. To such an extent that, at around 13 years old, a friend commissioned his first "architecture job" when he asked to design a house in a game. Little did he know, that that moment marked his path towards his profession.

Nuno da Graça Ribeiro is a fast learner and always looks for the most efficient way to create something. His stubbornness allows him to make near impossible things to happen. His interests extends beyond architecture, reaching the fields of engineering, astrophysics, chemistry, nutrition and psychology. Every challenge is accepted with an open mind while aiming to improve his knowledge of the world.
